IMPORT PROCESS

Step 1: Product Identification

Our team works closely with you to identify the specific products you need to import, and we source them for from trusted and reliable suppliers to ensure higher quality.

Step 2: Shipping & Logistics

We handle all aspects of the shipping and logistics process, including container loading, warehousing, and customs clearance. Our team ensures that your products are transported safely and efficiently to their destination.

Step 3: Delivery & Inspection

Upon arrival, our team conducts a thorough inspection of your products to ensure that they meet your quality. We then deliver them to your final destination, providing you with peace of mind and hassle free importing.

QUALITY CONTROL

We have a stringent quality control process implemented to assure quality and guarantee our customers lasting satisfaction.

  • We assess and vet the capability of our prospects and product manufacturers.
  • We only partner with quality-driven suppliers who provide assurance of conformance to global standards.
  • Our in-house quality control team regularly carries out further assessment of incoming products, including a visual and functional inspection, to ensure they meet our customer’s standards.
